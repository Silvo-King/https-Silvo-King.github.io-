Who We Are

Building with Purpose & Precision

Slychan Construction Limited is a premier construction firm based in Makadara, Nairobi, dedicated to delivering high-quality residential and commercial projects across Kenya. We combine modern design sensibility with rigorous engineering standards.

Every project we undertake is a reflection of our commitment to craftsmanship, transparency, and building relationships as strong as the structures we erect. From the first consultation to the final handover, we are with you every step of the way.
